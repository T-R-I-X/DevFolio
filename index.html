About Me

I started programming in 2014 at a young age to take my mind off the world. The language I began with was JavaScript, a goto web browser language. Since it was a friendly starting programming language, I hastily became fluent in it. My first full-time project was a locally hosted website that listed your TODOs for the day (It has helped my father and mother greatly; haha). I love programming. Overall programming has progressed from being a hobby to becoming a well loved passion; Allowing me to improve daily. I will never stop learning new and beautiful techniques to implement into my flavor of coding.
